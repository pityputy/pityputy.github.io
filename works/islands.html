Islands

Life is like an ocean.
And I am a little boat.
Sailing through all the Chaos, a great thunderstorm.
I throw the anchor overboard sometimes.
Spend some time on an island.
If I can see flowers blooming on its meadows as I sail by.
Sometimes I find one that is unique, small and hard to see from afar.
But I cannot ever stay forever.
I set sail again and again, but I never forget the islands and the seas.
I make a map in my head.
I would draw it, if I were a better artist.
And sometimes I use this map to go back.
Then I wonder how time flew by so fast.
Because the islands change a lot while I’m gone.
I wish I could find one that makes me want to stay.
One that has a lighthouse.
A lighthouse that draws me in, like a little boat in a storm.
A lighthouse where I can stay forever.
